Common AC Problems in Phoenix's Desert Climate

Compressor Failure

Phoenix's extreme heat puts enormous stress on AC compressors. Compressor failure is one of the most common AC problems we see, especially in older systems. Our technicians can diagnose compressor issues quickly and provide repair or replacement options to restore cooling.

Refrigerant Leaks

The constant expansion and contraction from Phoenix's temperature extremes can cause refrigerant leaks. Low refrigerant reduces cooling efficiency and can damage your compressor. We use advanced leak detection equipment to locate and repair leaks, then recharge your system with proper refrigerant levels.

Electrical Component Failures

Frequent cycling in Phoenix heat can cause electrical components to fail, including capacitors, contactors, and control boards. Our technicians carry common electrical components and can replace failed parts quickly to restore operation.

Frozen Evaporator Coils

Restricted airflow from dirty filters or blocked vents can cause evaporator coils to freeze, even in Phoenix heat. We identify the root cause of freezing and make necessary repairs to prevent recurrence.

Thermostat Malfunctions

Faulty thermostats can cause erratic operation or prevent your AC from running entirely. We test and calibrate thermostats or recommend smart thermostat upgrades for improved efficiency and control.
